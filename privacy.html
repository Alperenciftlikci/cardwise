Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

CardWise ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What Information CardWise Does NOT Collect

CardWise is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers. Here's what we specifically DO NOT collect:

  • Real Credit Card Numbers: CardWise never asks for or stores your actual credit card numbers, CVV codes, expiration dates, or any sensitive banking information
  • Personal Identification: No names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Transaction History: Your spending records and transaction data never leave your device
  • Location Data: We don't track or collect your location
  • Usage Analytics: No behavioral tracking, analytics, or telemetry data is collected
  • Device Identifiers: We don't collect device IDs or any hardware information

How CardWise Works - Complete Privacy

CardWise is designed to work entirely on your device without any server communication for your personal data:

What You Enter:

  • Card Nicknames Only: You only enter a name/label for each card (e.g., "Blue Cash Card", "Travel Rewards"). This is just for your own reference - we never see this information.
  • Cashback Rates: You manually set the cashback percentages for different spending categories. This information stays on your device only.
  • Transaction Amounts: When you log spending, only the amounts and categories are stored locally - no merchant names, locations, or other identifying details are required or transmitted.

Country/Region Selection:

CardWise allows you to select your country or region. This setting:

  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Is used to customize the app's spending categories to match local shopping patterns
  • Adjusts currency display and formatting
  • May enable region-specific card types and templates
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party

Your country selection is purely for enhancing your local experience - we have no way of knowing which country you selected.

Local Data Storage Only

All data you enter into CardWise is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure local storage (Core Data). This includes:

  • Card Information: Only the card nicknames you create and cashback rates you set
  • Transaction Records: Spending amounts and categories you manually log
  • Spending Insights: All statistics, charts, and recommendations are calculated locally on your device
  • App Settings: Your preferences, selected country/region, and app customizations

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to our servers, transmitted over the internet, or shared with anyone. The data is protected by your device's security measures, including encryption when your device is locked.

No Real Banking Information Required

CardWise is a manual tracking and recommendation tool, not a bank or financial institution. Here's what that means:

  • You are never asked to connect your actual bank accounts
  • You are never asked to provide real credit card numbers
  • You are never asked to enter CVV codes or security information
  • All card information is entered manually by you using nicknames of your choice
  • Transaction tracking is completely manual - you log what you want to track

This manual approach ensures maximum privacy: we can't collect what you don't provide, and you're in complete control of every piece of information in the app.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in the app settings, your CardWise data will be synced to your personal iCloud account. This feature:

  • Is entirely optional and disabled by default
  • Only syncs data to your private iCloud account
  • Is encrypted by Apple and never accessible to us
  • Can be disabled at any time in the app settings

We have no access to your iCloud data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles iCloud data.

Subscription and Payment Information

If you subscribe to CardWise Premium, your payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription status, but:

  • We never see your credit card or payment information
  • We only receive an anonymous identifier to verify your subscription
  • No personal information is shared with us

Apple's Privacy Policy governs how your payment information is handled.

Third-Party Services

CardWise uses minimal third-party services, and importantly, none of them receive your card data, transaction history, or personal information:

  • RevenueCat: Used solely for subscription management. Only an anonymous identifier is shared to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat never receives any information about your cards, spending, or transactions.
  • Apple Services:
    • App Store (for app distribution and in-app purchases)
    • iCloud (only if you explicitly enable it - see iCloud section below)
    • Push notifications (only if you enable them - used for optional spending reminders)

What we DON'T use:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks or ad tracking
  • No crash reporting tools that collect personal data
  • No social media integrations or tracking pixels
  • No location tracking services

Children's Privacy

CardWise is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data at all, this is guaranteed.

Data Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • All data staying on your device, protected by iOS security
  • Encryption when your device is locked
  • No transmission of sensitive information over the internet
  • No central servers storing your information

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at all times
  • Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all data from your device
  • Export: Premium users can export their data from within the app
  • Portability: Your data can be exported in standard formats

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or CardWise's privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: cardwise.app@gmail.com

Since we don't collect your data, most privacy concerns are automatically addressed by our architecture. However, we're happy to answer any questions you may have.

No Server-Side Data Processing

CardWise operates fundamentally differently from most financial apps:

  • No Data Transmission: Your card information, transactions, and spending data are never transmitted to any server, including ours
  • No Account Required: You don't create an account, so there's no server-side profile with your data
  • No Cloud Storage (unless you choose): By default, data stays on your device. If you enable iCloud, it goes to your personal iCloud, not our servers
  • All Processing Happens Locally: Cashback calculations, spending insights, card recommendations - everything is computed on your device using your device's processor

This architecture makes it technically impossible for us to access, collect, or misuse your financial data - because we never receive it in the first place.

Summary

The Bottom Line:

  • ✅ You enter only card nicknames, not real card numbers
  • ✅ All data is stored locally on your device
  • ✅ Your country selection is only used locally to customize the app
  • ✅ Nothing is sent to our servers - no tracking, no analytics, no data collection
  • ✅ We can't access your data even if we wanted to - we never receive it
  • ✅ Your financial information, spending habits, and personal preferences remain 100% private

CardWise is built with privacy by design. We believe your financial data is yours alone, and we've architected the app to ensure it stays that way.
